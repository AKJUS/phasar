Detailed Description

Similar to SparseLLVMBasedICFG; the only difference is that this one is no LLVMBasedICFG – it contains a pointer to an already existing one. It still owns the sparse value-flow graphs.

Use this in the IDESolver or IFDSSolver to profit from the SparseIFDS or SparseIDE optimization after Karakaya et al. "Symbol-Specific Sparsification of Interprocedural Distributive Environment Problems" https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2401.14813
