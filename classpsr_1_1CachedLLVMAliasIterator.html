Detailed Description

A wrapper over a LLVMAliasIteratorRef that adds caching to make it conform to the LLVMAliasInfoRef interface.

Note
Currently assumes that the underlying alias information is flow-insensitive and the granularity of different alias-information per instruction is actually at function-level
