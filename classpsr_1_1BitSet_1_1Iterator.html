Detailed Description

template<typename IdT, typename BitVectorTy = llvm::BitVector>
class psr::BitSet< IdT, BitVectorTy >::Iterator

Wraps BitVectorTy::const_set_bits_iterator, as LLVM's bitset iterators unfortunately do not conform to the named requirement of an iterator
